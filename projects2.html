Context

Aster, is a startup that seeks to improve women’s health practices. Pregnant individuals face challenges in managing healthcare documentation for prenatal and postpartum care, leading to incomplete or delayed submissions to the provider. Aster aims to create an easy-to-use, all-inclusive mobile platform to streamline this process and improve efficiency. I worked in a team of four to design an interface encouraging efficient and quick form completion.

Problem

How to establish a mobile app encouraging pregnant women to complete their healthcare forms before a physical check-up?

Anticipated Audience

Pregnant Pauline: Pauline is a first-time expectant mother with a full-time job. She is under immense stress and often feels overwhelmed with the task of filling out multiple forms. However, she also hates wasting time in the waiting room trying to fill out all the paperwork.

Doctor Dan: Dr.Dan is an OBGYN who sees 20+ patients daily. He struggles to organize the numerous paperwork to show healthcare providers - each of whom have different requirements. He often has to add in each patient’s information manually.

Features in Lo-Fi

User Choice Experience

The user has the option to choose from a game, affirmation or standard route to form completion

Notification Pop-Ups

Users can fill out questions through a notification pop-up on their lock screen

Progress Bars

Users can track how much of the form is completed and how much is remaining

Indications of time required for each form

Users can see how long each form will take before they begin filling it out to prevent feelings of overwhelm and stress

Mascot

An Aster Mascot that encourages user to complete the form and provides positive affirmations

Final Critique

We presented the data to Aster Founders and received an immensely positive response. They cited features that will be added to the product

Scan Feature

Aster Founders had never thought of this feature nor seen it be implemented but believe it could streamline the process for the providers - who are their target user

Voice Note/Call

Founders cited it was a unique idea that could humanise the app and create a warm aura

Aster Assistant

Founders appreciated chatbot integration was seamless and streamlined the entire process

Notification Popups

Founders believed it saved time and gently encouraged users

Time Progress Bars

This helped to remove the patient’s stress of spending too much time on forms
